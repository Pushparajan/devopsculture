Anthony McLin is a Director of Experience Engineering at SapientRazorfish. He oversees frontend architecture and development delivery across multiple teams and agencies for a Fortune 500 retailing enterprise. For more than five years, Anthony has been helping this client push and mature their online platform to deliver over 200 sites for more than 10 well-known consumer apparel brands.

Anthony has over twenty years experience delivering design-centric websites and digital communications for high-profile architects, designers, publications, gaming companies, and ecommerce retailers. As a part of SapientRazorfish's CMTOu executive leadership program, he has spent nine months doing deep research about the DevOps space, to identify ways to drive it forward not just for his clients and employer, but for the technology community as a whole.
